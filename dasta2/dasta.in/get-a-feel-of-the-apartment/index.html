Get a feel of the apartment

29th October, 2014

No matter how specific and elaborate we get with orienting you with Dasta Concerto online, getting a physical feel for the space is essential. The Model Apartment is an ideal experience to spatially orient yourself with the scale and functionality of the apartment. We’ve built a typical 3 BHK apartment and furnished it to give you an idea of what can possibly be accomplished in your own home. The Model Apartment is located at the site, within the on-site Marketing Office.
