Oct 2014 Report

17th October, 2014
Construction Update

As of October 2014, construction has reached the 5th floor slab. Progress at the Dasta Concerto site is at an ideal state; we are 15 days ahead of our scheduled date of completion. In our effort to bring you transparency and convenience, we’ve put together this construction report to help you get an idea of what’s happening at the site. The photo above should serve as a simple visual progress indicator. You can visually track Slab and Block Work progress using it.

In order to better understand whats happening on site, you will need to do a bit of reading. We have tried to simplify this by sticking to a point form list.

Here is the breakdown of the current construction progress :

What have we completed?

● The slabs for the Ground, 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th & 5th floor have been cast.
● Block work on the Ground & 1st floor is completed.

What is in progress?

● The 6th floor columns are in progress and the slab will follow soon.
● Block work on the 2nd & 3rd floor is in progress.
● Concrete work on the Sewage Treatment Pump, the Underground Ground Sump Tank and the Clubhouse is in progress.
● Ramp work and Ground floor entrance slab marking is in progress.
● Punning work with 90 degree wall markings at the Ground floor is in progress.
● Ceiling work at Ground floor is in progress.
● The procurement of electrical components such as Switch Boxes and Distribution Boards are in progress.
● Electrical work like roof checking, wall chasing, conduit laying and switch box fixing are in progress.
● Plumbing material procurement and basement ceiling works are in progress.
● Shop drawing for Fire Protection for basement is approved and the procurement is in progress.
● Waterproofing material procurement is complete and work has started.
● Floor tile markings are in progress up to the 3rd floor.
● Toilet wall plastering material has been procured.
