October 2015 Report

3rd November, 2015
Construction Update

 As of October 2015, Block work has been completed on the 14th Floor Slab. The photo above should serve as a simple visual progress indicator. This report is structured like our last report to make it easy for you to track progress.

Here is the breakdown of the current construction progress

What have we completed?

• East & West wing OHT work is completed.
• Internal block work at Ground till terrace floor is completed.
• Door jambs, touch ups, kitchen area POP and punning work in 1st till 13th floor flats is completed.
• Door frames in the flats fixed up to 4th floor.
• Electrical model boxes in the Ground, 1st till 13th floor flats have been installed.
• Marking & chasing for plumbing pipes in Ground, 1st till 13th floor is completed.
• Bore packing of sanitary lines from 1st till 12th floor is completed.
• Sanitary lines in the Ground, 1st till 12th floor ceiling has been completed.
• Corridor plastering work from Ground, 1st till 13th floor has been completed.
• Staircase railing on the east & west wing is completed from Ground to the terrace floor.
• Fire staircase door-frames have been fixed on the Ground, 1st up to 13th floor.
• East wing staircase plastering work from Ground, 1st till 12th floor is completed.
• Waterproofing of toilet areas at Ground, 1st till 12th floor flats has been completed.
• Screed concrete work of toilet area & kitchen utility in the flats has been completed from Ground, 1st till 12th floor.
• Screed concrete work of balcony area has been completed from 1st till 10th floor.
• Toilet dado tile work from 1st till 12th floor has been completed.
• Toilet floor tile work from 1st till 12th floor has been completed.
• Individual flat floor tile work has been completed from 1st till 12th floor.
• Balcony floor tile works from 1st till 5th floor has been completed.
• Screed concrete work in master bed rooms of the flat has been completed from 1st till 12th floor.
• Structure ready for sewage treatment plant, underground sump tank, club house & swimming pool.
• Swimming pool water proofing work is completed.
• Jalli is been fixed in the balcony of individual flats up to 13th floor.
• External plastering work is completed up to 10th floor.
• Plastering work for the extended cantilever slab in the 13th floor is completed.
• South side compound wall work is completed.
• Basement plumbing ceiling lines work is completed.
