Jan 2015 Report

3rd February, 2015
Construction Update

As of January 2015, construction has reached the 10th floor slab.The photo above should serve as a simple visual progress indicator. This report is structured like our last report to make it easy for you to track progress.

Here is the breakdown of the current construction progress

What have we completed?

● The slabs for the Ground, 1st till 10th Floor have been cast.
● Block work activity from Ground, 1st till 7th floor is completed.
● POP punning work from 1st till 3rd floor is completed.
● Electrical model boxes for the Ground, 1st till 3rd floor have been completed.
● Marking & Chasing works for Plumbing pipes have been completed from Ground, 1st till 5th floor have been completed.
● Staircase Railing from Ground floor till 6th floor have been completed.

What is in progress?

● Concrete work in club house and swimming pool is underway.
● Block work on 8th floor is in progress.
● Electrical model boxes for the 4th, 5th and 6th floor is in progress.
● Punning work on the 4th floor is in progress.
● Plumbing work at 3rd, 4th, 5th , Basement ceiling and flooring works are in progress.
● Waterproofing of toilet areas of Ground and 1st floor is in progress.
● Mockup Cement Jali sample work is in progress.
