Why work with us?

With Fueb, you get the benefits of working with an independent OEM and our in-depth understanding of the home automation market, both local & global.

Service & Support

Our products are designed for easy installation & maintenance, but our team of trained professionals is always available to resolve any issues that may arise.

Constant Innovation

We design and manufacture all our products in-house. Our adaptive R&D team is always on the lookout for the next innovation.

Affordable, Accessible

We aim to make home automation ubiquitous and cost-effective, so that we are at the front of consumers’ minds when they make their choice.

R&D Center

At our Bengaluru-based R&D center, innovation is our priority. We design, develop and test all our products in-house, simplifying the process of adding customisations or updates as compared to other manufacturers.

We focus on creating high quality, cost-effective and easy-to-use products that are at the forefront of the home automation market

OEM

As an OEM, we ensure that our manufacturing units follows the most stringent procedures with a focus on quality control and innovation. It also helps us to provide our customers with unmatched service, including a hassle-free after sales experience and longer product warranty periods.

We’ve also developed a wide and well-trained dealership presence that is there to meet the needs of our customers at all times.

Service & Support

Our highly trained team is always available to provide any kind of after sales service that may be required. Being an OEM, we know our products inside out , which gives us the ability to deliver customer support without having to rely on any third party providers.

By integrating this strong service team with our vast dealership network, we aim to be just a call away anytime you need our help.
