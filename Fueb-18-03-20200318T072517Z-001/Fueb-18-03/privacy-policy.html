Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Fueb Labs Automations Pvt. Ltd. and its subsidiaries, affiliates and parent companies (collectively, "we") collect, use, share and protect information obtained from the services, websites, and applications offered by FUEB (the "Service"), including www.fueblabs.com and www.fueb.io (the "Sites").

Information We Collect:

When you visit our Sites, we may ask you for personal information to provide a service or carry out a transaction that you have requested. You may also provide personal information to us when you contact us (e.g., with a comment, inquiry or customer support request) or register for an account to use our Sites. The Service does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13 years old.

How we use your personal data:

We may use your personal information to deliver services to you or carry out transactions you have requested, including, but not limited to, providing information on Company products or services you have purchased or otherwise use, registering purchased products, processing product orders, handling warranty claims, replacing product manuals, answering customer service requests and facilitating the use of our Sites.

Marketing in accordance with applicable laws and regulations, we may use your personal information to inform you of products or services which may be of interest to you, and to otherwise communicate with you about offerings, events and news, surveys, special offers, and related topics

We and members of our group may use this information for the purpose of employment consideration.

Enforcement

We may use the personal information we collect in order to detect, prevent and respond to fraud, intellectual property infringement, violations of our terms and conditions, violations of law or other misuse of our Sites.
